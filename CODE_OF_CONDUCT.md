# Code of Conduct

We want Productside repositories to be useful, respectful places to collaborate and learn.

## Our standard

Please be respectful, constructive, and generous in how you participate.

That includes:

- assuming good intent while staying honest about problems
- giving feedback on ideas and work without attacking people
- helping make the path clearer for others
- avoiding harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior

## Scope

This applies in issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces managed through this organization.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that does not belong here, contact: `support@productside.com`

## Enforcement

The organization owners and maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when needed to protect the health of the community.

## Legal

Participation in Productside repositories is subject to Productside's [Terms & Conditions](https://productside.com/terms-conditions/). Reports submitted under this Code of Conduct are handled in accordance with our [Privacy Policy](https://productside.com/privacy-policy/).
