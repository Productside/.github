Productside helps Product Managers and product teams build better products through practical training, proven frameworks, and hands-on advisory support.

This GitHub organization is where we publish selected tools, templates, examples, and working artifacts that support modern product practice — including AI-shaped product work.

---

## Practical by design

Productside is built around a simple idea: great products do not happen by chance. They are built by teams that understand their customers, align around outcomes, and know how to execute.

That same standard applies here. We publish materials that help teams do the work, not just talk about it:

- practical tools and reference implementations
- [Playbook](https://productside.com/playbooks/)-derived assets and reusable workflows
- templates and examples that support real product decisions
- selected experiments related to [AI Product Management](https://productside.com/ai-product-management-training-course/)

## What you should expect from this GitHub org

We care about practical usefulness over novelty theater. That means:

- clear workflows over mysterious prompt magic
- durable artifacts over one-off demos
- product judgment over tool hype
- examples people can adapt without needing insider context

## Current focus

Productside publishes deliberately — not everything, and not before it is ready. Some repositories are private or in progress.

When something is published here, the goal is for it to be useful enough that a real product team can learn from it, adapt it, or put it to work.

---

## A note on AI

Productside uses AI where it helps teams think better, move faster, and preserve useful context. We are interested in stronger discovery, clearer decisions, better artifacts, and more leverage for people doing real product work — not AI for its own sake.
